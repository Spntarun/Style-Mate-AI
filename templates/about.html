About Us

Redefining Fashion with AI Intelligence

Style Mate bridges the gap between online shopping uncertainty and confident style choices through personalized recommendations and AI-powered visualization.

The Problem We Solve

Traditional online fashion shopping lacks personalization. Customers struggle with incorrect size selection, unsuitable outfit choices, and no way to visualize how clothes look on their body type before purchasing — leading to high return rates and dissatisfaction.

Our Solution

Style Mate analyzes user-specific attributes including height, weight, body measurements, body type, skin tone, gender, occasion, climate, and budget through a rule-based expert system. Combined with Style Mate AI visualization, users get realistic outfit previews tailored to their unique profile.
